OVERVIEW!


Pastor - Ronnie Bruce Gregory

Hi, My name is Ronnie Bruce Gregory. My wife's name was Rebecca, Jo Martin Gregory. She went home to be with her Lord September 10, 2016. We ministered to churches in 3 different states together for over 45 years. I attended Sunset School Peaching in Lubbock, Texas and have been preaching for more than 52 years now. I have been blessed to be preaching and ministering to the First Christian Church in Elgin, Texas for the last 15 years come Oct. 11, 2024. I preach and teach from the Bible only. I pray and strive with my very being to do it with love and honesty to honor God only.
